About us

The “Medic Hero 4” is a NLP text based AI web chatbot which helps people by providing symptomatic assistance in medical consultancy, quick home remedies ,diet charts , searching nearby hospitals , recommending specialist Doctors according to user’s condition. The service is freely available on web browser or similar services and can be accessed through mobile or personal computers. Our chat bot can identify symptoms to leading problems and provide genuine one to one personalized medical consultations and suggestions to users with a reliable accuracy.
